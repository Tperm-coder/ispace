About

  • August 2017

    A Recognized Need

    Being exposed to different market trends in Cairo, we started to recognize the need of a coworking space in 15 May City. A place where people could meet to work and/or study, and for entrepreneurs to build their startups, was highly needed especially in the presence of an institute of higher education.

  • September 2017

    Ideas to Images

    From scattered imaginations to organized planning, the talented architect along with his team, Abdullah Mahmoud, was able to transform the idea into an architectural design. From the layout of the space to the choice of furniture and artistic touches, i Space was coming emerging.

  • January 2018

    The Opening

    In the 30th of January, i Space was officially established and was ready to serve the community. Growing up as a family, i Space hosted a lot of events, workshops, and startups along its journey.

  • January 2018 - now

    Community Impact

    Through its journey, i Space was blessed with its unique community. Celebrating community members' professional & personal achievements is our family motto.
